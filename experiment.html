SHRI VISHNU ENGINEERING COLLEGE FOR WOMEN


Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women is a was founded by Sri Vishnu Educational Society in 2001. The college is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Kakinada since 2011 and was accredited in 2008 by NBA for 5 years. The college offers Bachelor and Master Courses in various field of Technology. It also offers Professional Programme in Business and Computer Application. SVECW has been chosen as the Best Consumer of APEPDCL in West Godavari District for use of alternative energy resources. The college has also been granted autonomous status by UGC. SVECW has an area of 100 acres consists of building, residence, auditorium, playgrounds, colleges or parking. The institute was awarded the Global Performance Excellence Award by Asia Pacific Quality Organization (APQO) on 15th October 2012 SVECW Ranking
